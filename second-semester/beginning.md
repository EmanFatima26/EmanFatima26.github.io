# 🌸 Beginning of Second Semester

> **Second Semester Journey | Chapter 1**

---

Armed with a highly satisfying GPA from my freshman semester, I stepped into the second semester with a heart full of grand ambitions and a mind charged with raw enthusiasm. The initial transition period into university life was officially over, and my goals were crystal clear: I wanted to dedicate the entirety of my energy to core programming, master advanced coding workflows, and expand my technical horizons as much as humanly possible.

However, as the formal timetable rolled out, we discovered a major reshuffling in our academic faculty. Our Head of Department (HOD), Sir Akram, was assigned to take over our advanced object-oriented classes, while our absolute favorite instructor, **Dr. Bilal**, was handed the reins of our Database Systems course.

Knowing his profound, industry-grade expertise in Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), and advanced database architecture, the whole class instantly knew we were in for an intellectually stimulating and highly practical ride.
